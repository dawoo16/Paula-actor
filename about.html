Born on 12. June 1995 in Stargard, Poland. Moved to Norway at age 16. Graduated with a law degree in 2022 and became a licensed attorney in 2025.

Began acting journey in school theatre, followed by further development through a series of acting workshops in Poland. Since 2024, a member of the Actors Hub Studio, with regular training in screen acting and ongoing mentorship from acclaimed director David Nutter. In 2024/2025, completed workshops led by industry professionals such as Paul Johansson (“American TV Series,” “On Camera Masterclass”) and Matthew Hastings (“Talent Search”), including a workshop in Los Angeles hosted and led by David Nutter. The craft is constantly complemented by classes and coaching, such as those at The Lee Strasberg Theatre & Film Institute, Lesly Kahn & Co, and others.

Interesting fact: In 2024, participated in the “Top Model Europe” competition and reached the semi-final stage.
