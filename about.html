She is a qualified lawyer, having earned her attorney title in 2025.

She is fluent in Polish (native), English, and Norwegian, and has basic knowledge of Spanish.

She has been developing her craft through a range of acting trainings and workshops in methods such as Strasberg, Demidov and Meisner. She is also trained in the genre of comedy. Her training includes studies at The Lee Strasberg Theatre & Film Institute, Lesly Kahn & Company, LSA – Les Ateliers Professionnels de l’Acteur, Stanisławski Studio and Meisner Institute Poland.

She has taken part in workshops in Los Angeles, Paris and Warsaw. She learned from industry professionals such as acclaimed director David Nutter, actor Paul Johansson and renowned acting coach Lola Cohen. Since 2024, a member of the Actors’ Hub practicing screen acting as part of acting classes.

She is skilled in horseback riding, swimming, cycling, table tennis, winter sports, and fitness training, has basic knowledge of Latin and Standard dance, plays piano (self-taught, beginner), and holds a valid driving license.
